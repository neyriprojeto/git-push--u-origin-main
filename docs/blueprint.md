# **App Name**: AdKairos Connect

## Core Features:

- Member Management: Manage member data, including personal information and group affiliations.
- Digital ID: Generate digital ID cards for members.
- Member Profile: Create detailed member profiles with personal information, history, and participation records.
- Church Banner: Displays the church's banner at the top of the home page, with a picture of the leading pastor.
- About Info: Display 'about' and 'pastoral message' from the pastor on the homepage.
- Platform versatility: Ability to function on any and all major OS platforms

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5) to convey trust and stability, inspired by the traditional and community aspects of church.
- Background color: Light blue-gray (#ECEFF1) to provide a calm and neutral backdrop.
- Accent color: Warm orange (#FFAB40) to highlight important actions and elements, contrasting the cool blues for visual interest.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, suitable for both headlines and body text, easy to read on screens.
- Headline font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif
- Use clear, modern icons for navigation and member actions.
- Simple and intuitive layout, with clear navigation for easy access to member information and features.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience without distracting from the content.
